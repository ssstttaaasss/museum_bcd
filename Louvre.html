Louvre Museum

  • History and Collections
  • Iconic Exhibits
  • Planning Your Visit

    • History and Collections : The Louvre Museum in Paris is the largest art museum in the world, attracting millions of visitors each year. Housed in the Louvre Palace, the museum is renowned for its impressive collection and architecture.

    Exhibits : The museum boasts astonishing masterpieces of world art, such as Leonardo da Vinci's Mona Lisa, the Venus de Milo, and many other renowned works.

    Planning Your Visit : For a better visit, it is recommended to book tickets in advance, use an audio guide, or take a tour to gain a deeper understanding of the history and significance of each exhibit.

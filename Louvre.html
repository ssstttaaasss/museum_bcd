Collections:
The Louvre has one of the largest and most diverse art collections in the world, including masterpieces of both ancient, medieval, renaissance and modern art.

Educational role:
The museum offers a variety of tours, lectures and educational programs for visitors of various age groups, helping to reveal the richness of its collections.

Reason to visit:
If you are interested in art from different eras and cultures, the Louvre will be an unforgettable place for you.

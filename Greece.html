Collections:
The museum has the largest collection of archaeological finds in Greece, including sculptures, vases, coins and other artefacts.

Educational role:
Exhibitions and tours allow visitors to learn more about ancient Greek history and culture.

Reason to visit:
If you are interested in ancient history and culture, the museum in Athens offers a unique immersive experience.

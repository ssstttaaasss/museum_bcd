  • Planning Your Visit

    • History and Collections: The Greek Museum stands as one of the most significant museums globally, dedicated to ancient Greek civilization. Established in 1866, it boasts an impressive collection of artifacts reflecting the richness and diversity of ancient Greek culture.

    Iconic Exhibits: The museum houses unique masterpieces such as statues, vases, mosaics, and other art objects that testify to the influence and significance of ancient Greek civilization.

    Planning Your Visit: Visitors can enjoy guided tours allowing them to better understand the historical context and culture of ancient Greece and appreciate the immense contribution of this civilization to world art.
